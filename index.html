Catch Word

In this game you have to collect as many score as you can. There are seven stages from easiest to hardest which is called 'Final Stage' and it's hardest of them all. every other stage got lower time cooldown so that makes it harder. You have to type as fast as you can and hit enter that will add time to counter and make game easier for you. Compete to other players. Good luck!
